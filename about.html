My Story

Hello, I'm Brian Westfall

For over two decades, my passion has been helping people create the outdoor oasis they've always dreamed of. From owning a pool construction company to hands-on building, I've been involved in every phase of the process — design, construction, outdoor living spaces, and everything in between.

My journey brought me to Fort Worth, where I met my incredible wife, Julie. Her inspiration has been the foundation for this chapter of my career, allowing me to focus on the part of the process I love most: the design.

Today I work as an AI Visual Design Artist, combining my deep construction knowledge with state-of-the-art technology to help clients see and experience their vision before a single stone is laid. My sophisticated yet approachable design process is built on a simple foundation: to reflect your aspirations and enhance your lifestyle.

I also hold a Texas All-Lines Insurance Adjuster License and conduct swimming pool inspections — bringing the same attention to detail and commitment to safety that drives every project I touch.

I enhance projects by incorporating natural elements — light, water, and location — to create a space that perfectly reflects my clients' desires. By extensively integrating unique aquatic features, my designs stand out and deliver that "wow factor" you're seeking.
