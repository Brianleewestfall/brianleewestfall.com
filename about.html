My Story

Hello, I'm Brian Westfall

For over two decades, my passion has been helping people create the outdoor oasis they've always dreamed of. After years of hands-on experience owning a successful pool construction company, my journey brought me to Fort Worth, where I met my incredible wife, Julie. Her inspiration has been the foundation for this chapter of my career, allowing me to focus on the part of the process I love most: the design.

I now combine my deep understanding of construction with state-of-the-art technology to help my clients see and experience their vision before a single stone is laid.

Understanding my client's unique tastes and aspirations is the heart of what I do. My sophisticated yet approachable design process is built on a simple foundation: to reflect your aspirations and enhance your lifestyle.

I enhance projects by incorporating natural elements — light, water, and location — to create a space that perfectly reflects my clients' desires. By extensively integrating unique aquatic features, my designs stand out and deliver that "wow factor" you're seeking.
